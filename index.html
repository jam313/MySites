Purpose

So, this summer is [hopefully] my final summer as an undergraduate student. I am taking classes during the second summer semester, so I have decided to use the first semester to learn more about various aspects of the computer science field. One of my main projects will be to contruct a slick website in which I can display my resumé, ongoing and finished projects, and possibly a link to a GitHub repository or two that I feel I can be proud of. Hopefully, it will all come together and be an awesome asset to later show potential employers, potential clients, and friends and family.
